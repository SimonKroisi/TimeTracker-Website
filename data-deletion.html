How to Request Data Deletion

Follow these simple steps within the TimeTracker app to request deletion of specific data:

1

Open Settings

Open the TimeTracker app and navigate to Settings (gear icon in the menu).

2

Go to Support / Help

In the Settings menu, tap on Support / Help or Privacy Settings.

3

Create a New Support Request

Tap on Create New Support Request or Contact Support.

4

Specify What to Delete

Clearly describe which data you want to delete. Be specific about:

  • Type of data (entries, projects, files, etc.)
  • Date range (if applicable)
  • Specific items (if deleting individual records)
5

Submit Your Request

Submit the request and include your registered email address for verification.

üìß Alternative: Contact via Email

You can also request data deletion by sending an email to timetracker.track@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and specify what data you want removed.

Data Processing Timeline

  • Request Processing: 7-10 days for simple deletion requests
  • Complex Requests: Up to 30 days for requests affecting multiple projects or large datasets
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirming what data has been deleted
  • Your Account: Remains active and you can continue using TimeTracker normally

Automatic Data Deletion (No Action Required)

TimeTracker automatically cleans up certain data to respect your privacy:

  • Temporary Data: Session tokens and temporary caches are automatically cleared
  • Old Logs: Server logs are automatically purged after 30 days
  • Backup Cleanup: Deleted data is removed from backups within 90 days

Related Options

For different privacy needs:

  • Delete Your Entire Account: Permanently remove your account and all associated data
