Delete Your Account

How to request deletion of your TimeTracker account and data

How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your TimeTracker account and all associated data, please follow these simple steps within the app:

1

Open Settings

Open the TimeTracker app and navigate to Settings (gear icon in the menu).

2

Go to Support / Help

In the Settings menu, tap on Support / Help.

3

Create a New Support Request

Tap on Create New Support Request or Contact Support.

4

Request Account Deletion

In your message, clearly state that you want to delete your account and all associated data. Include your registered email address for verification.

üìß Alternative: Contact via Email

You can also request account deletion by sending an email to timetracker.track@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, profile picture, and login credentials
  • Time Tracking Data: All recorded work time entries and timestamps
  • Project Data: Projects you created or own (shared projects will remain for other team members)
  • Tasks and Activities: All tasks, activity logs, and associated records
  • Media and Files: Uploaded images, videos, audio messages, and documents
  • User Preferences: Settings, preferences, and notification configurations

Data Retention After Deletion

Please note the following retention periods after your deletion request is processed:

  • Account Data: Deleted within 30 days of request confirmation
  • Backup Data: May exist in backups for up to 90 days after deletion
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics data in Firebase Analytics may be retained for up to 14 months
  • Server Logs: System logs containing your activity may be retained for up to 30 days

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion. If you are part of a team, your shared project contributions may be retained for other team members.

Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

Questions?
